Delivery methods
The courier can deliver the order directly to your home.

Order payment
Payment to the courier upon receipt.
Payment by credit card - you can pay for the order with a visa or master card immediately after placing and order through the site or upon receipt of the goods. The courier will have a terminal.
Payment by receipt - to pay, you need to print and pay the receipt, and then send us a scanned copy of the received payment document to sales@example.com
Cashless payment (by account) - this calculation is most suitable for organizations. you place an order, print out the invoice, pay for it and call us to clarify the date of receipt of the order.
